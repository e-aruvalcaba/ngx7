Progress Bar

Uploading Progress Bar

These bars are for progress and success of a document upload.
When the user is uploading a document, they will see color and progress tracking for the status of the upload.

Green will mean the document has been successfully and fully uploaded while blue will indicate the document is in the process of being uploaded and there will be a time stamp under the bar indicating the remaining time left.
