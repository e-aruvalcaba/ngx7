Dashboard Modules

Used on dashboards or special situations, modules are used to display high level information, giving the user a view of the overall current state.

Modules are designed to:

1. Use very sparingly, and for high level actions.

2. Proactively serve up information and actions.

3. Visually display information with graphs and maps, accompanied by text and data.

4. Be a point of entry into any area of major functionality within an experience.
