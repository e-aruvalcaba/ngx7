CEMEX Inline Dialogue

The pin dialogue can be used on map overlays when tracking where a truck is and what contents the truck is carrying.

The dialogue should only contain the most pertinent information and should not have more than 6 line items.

Because of the nature of when someone would be viewing a truck delivery (on the same day, in real time) a date field is unnecessary.

If the user wants to close out of the dialogue pin, they can simply click anywhere else on the map, therefore no close icon is needed.
