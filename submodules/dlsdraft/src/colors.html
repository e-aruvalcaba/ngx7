Colors

Representative palette established in Flugee, the CEMEX Design Language, application experiences have a primary palette of blues, using CEMEX red as an accent.

Incorprating an element of freshness, also established in Flugee, vibrant colors are used sparingly throughout.

For status there are four primary colors used:

1. Red: Red is used to highlight an item that is either overdue or needs immediate attention (ie: an overdue invoice, a delayed delivery, etc).

2. Bright Blue: Bright blue is used as the main active link + button color, and is also used to denote something that is pending because the customer has not confirmed a piece of information (ie: CEMEX has proposed a new delivery time, and the customer needs to confirm that the time works for them).

3. Bright Orange: Bright orange is used as the main pending color throughout the experience. This is used when the user has done any actions they are required, but are simply waiting for CEMEX to confirm a piece of information for them (ie if they have submitted a new jobsite and CEMEX needs to confirm the new location, if they have requested an order but CEMEX needs to confirm the quantity and delivery time)

4. Bright Green: This green is used to show when an item is active or confirmed, CEMEX has done any confirmation of information they needed to and so has the customer. It is also used in buttons to show progression and the ability to move onto the next item.

5. Grey Disabled: This shade of grey is used to show that an item is in-active or disabled.
